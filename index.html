how does nabii logistics work?

Our network of local sales agent promotes the transportation services to Congo owners and shippers. Once the transportation service agreement is formalized with a Congo owner trucks are selected, inspected with all data stored on the platform, Congo owners can start booking a shipper and have access to the pricing, trucks inspection report, capacity tonnage of each shippers before selecting the most appropriate. In real time quotes are generated from the platform and directed to shippers. Then shippers are notified and can negotiate the quote through the platform.

Once the negotiation is completed, the platform generates a contract the shippers can them start the journey. once the trip is tarted, the Congo owners are able to track in real time where the trucks are. Shippers can also provide daily tracking events such as incident tracking until they reach final destination. Once the POD is loaded on the platform, the shippers will receive its final payment. Cargo owner and shippers can rate their own experience.

What is nabii logistics?

Nabii logistics is a haulage exchange platform with a mobile application, that connects cargo owners and shippers to bring transparency and efficiency

Why using nabii logistics?

The platform ensures that cargo owners and shippers can do the following:
  • Negotiate decent transportation rates
  • Optimize the transportation journey with backloads
  • Rate and review the quality of service deliver
  • Access information about shippers
  • Offering background checks and keeping vehicles up-to date

Where do we operate?

Our headquarter is in DRC, Lubumbashi with agents in Namibia and south Africa. We offer a regional perspective: acting on 4 corridors: DRC /Tanzania, DRC/south Africa, DRC/Namibia, DRC/Mozambique.

WHICH PRODUCTS CAN BE LOADED?

We are delivering different goods such as lime, cement, steel, copper, wood, food or beverages.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST TO USE NABII LOGISTICS?

We are charging between 5-12% of the quote of each transaction depending on the corridor and goods loaded.
